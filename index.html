How Does It Work?

Once you're register at Found.Today, you get a tag which you can put on all your valuable items. If someone finds your tagged belonging, they can scan the Found.Today QR code and quickly get a message to you. The message they send and your reply back will be handled by Found.Today without disclosing the senders or recipients email addresses.

What Do I Need?

If you see a tag, scan the QR code with your smart phone and send a message. Each tag also comes with a webaddress which you can use to send a message in case you don't have a QR reader app.
